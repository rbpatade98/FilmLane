Help Center

Account & Subscription

- To create an account, click "Sign In" and register with your email.

- Manage your plan in Account Settings > My Subscription.

Streaming Issues

- Clear your browser cache and ensure your internet speed is above 5 Mbps.

- Switch to a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

App Support

- Download the FilmLane app on Android and iOS for a smoother experience and offline viewing.

Login Problems

- Forgot password? Use the password reset link on the login page.

- If you’re locked out, contact support@filmlane.com.
