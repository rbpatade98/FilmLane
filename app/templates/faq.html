Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start watching movies?

Sign in or create an account, then click on any “Watch Now” button under the movie or series to start streaming instantly.

Is FilmLane free to use?

We offer a free tier with limited content access. For full access to HD and exclusive releases, a premium subscription is required.

Can I download content for offline viewing?

Yes, premium members can download select movies and episodes through our mobile app for offline viewing.

What devices are supported?

FilmLane works on all modern browsers, Android/iOS phones, tablets, and Smart TVs (including Chromecast & Firestick).

How do I reset my password?

Go to the sign-in page and click on "Forgot Password." A password reset link will be sent to your registered email.

Who do I contact for billing issues?

Email our support team at billing@filmlane.com with your registered email and transaction details.
